As part of the 2021-2022 Praxis Program cohort in the UVa Library Scholars' Lab, I collaborated with an amazing and inspiring group of fellows as well as Slab staff to create an OER that guides graduate students through creating their own personal website. I used this project to help me make this site.

My archival research relies heavily on zines accessed in the private collections of scene participants that are not housed in “official” archives. I am at work building a digital archive that will make these publications more accessible to a broader audience of future utopian dreamers and radical historians. My obsession with zines has spilled over into pedagogy and I designed a workshop for using zines to teach digital humanities methods. If you or someone you know has a Detroit-related zine collection, please contact me!

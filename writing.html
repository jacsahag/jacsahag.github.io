Grant Writing

Doctoral Research

  • RBS-UVA Fellowship, Rare Book School. This grant provides tuition funding plus a $250 stipend to participate in a summer course at the Rare Book School. For this fellowship, I had to write a letter to the course instructor and a short essay explaining how the course content would complement my research.
  • Bordin-Gillette Researcher Travel Fellowship, University of Michigan Bentley Historical Library. This grant provides $2k in travel funds to do archival research at the University of Michigan's Bentley Historical Library. Here is my application.
  • Dolores Zohrab Liebmann Fellowship. In 2022 I received this national fellowship from the Dolores Liebmann Fund, which provides $18k of support a year for 3 years. Here is my statement of purpose.
  • UVa Scholars' Lab Praxis Program Fellowship. For the 2021-2022 academic year I was a Praxis Program fellow in the Scholars' Lab, which bought me out of teaching responsibilities and taught me skills in the digital humanities. Applicants must write a letter of interest.
  • UVa Arts, Humanities, and Social Sciences Summer Research Grant. This grant through the Graduate School of the Arts, Humanities, and Social Sciences at UVa provides funding up to $5k for summer research. Applicants must submit a project statement and a budget.
  • UVa Graduate School of Arts and Sciences Council Research Grant. The GSASC awards up to $750 to support graduate student research. Applicants must submit a research statement including a budget and a feasibility statement.

Work

Outside my academic work, I have over a decade of varied professional writing, library, and nonprofit experience. I am passionate about the role of libraries in creating a more socially just and liberated society, and have worked in public and university library systems in both Michigan and Virginia.

I have additionally done grants administration, membership coordination, and editorial work with nonprofits including:

  • United Campus Workers Virginia
